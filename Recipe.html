Classic Homemade Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4–6 lemons)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices (for garnish)
  • Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Juice the lemons until you get about 1 cup of fresh lemon juice.
  2. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice and sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves fully.
  3. Add the cold water and stir to mix well.
  4. Taste and adjust the sweetness or tartness as desired.
  5. Add ice cubes and lemon slices into glasses, then pour in the lemonade.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves if desired. Serve chilled and enjoy!
Cheers! Enjoy your refreshing homemade lemonade.
